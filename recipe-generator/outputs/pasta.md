# pasta

Here's a simple recipe for a delicious pasta dish:

**Classic Pasta Recipe**

**Ingredients:**
• 8 oz (225g) pasta of your choice (e.g., spaghetti, linguine, or fettuccine)
• 2 tbsp olive oil
• 1 small onion, finely chopped
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 cup (250g) cherry tomatoes, halved
• 1 tsp dried basil
• Salt and pepper to taste
• Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent (about 3-4 minutes).
3. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
4. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes, until they start to release their juices.
5. Season with dried basil, salt, and pepper to taste.
6. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the skillet with the tomato mixture. Toss everything together.
7. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy!
